## 3Ô∏è‚É£ Trading Section (Internal Trades Management)

A dedicated **Trading Section** for internal teams (agents, managers, CRM admins) with the following capabilities:

* **Trade Creation**

  * Users (agents/team leaders/CRM managers) can **open trades for clients** (single or multiple clients at once).
  * Ability to **select symbol/instrument**, volume, order type (Market/Limit/Stop), and optional notes.

* **Trade Editing**

  * Full edit capabilities for open and closed trades, including:

    * P&L adjustments
    * Opening/closing time
    * Opening/closing price
    * Spread, fees, or bonuses
  * All trade edits must be **audited** with timestamp and user performing the action.

* **Batch Operations**

  * Ability to **close multiple trades simultaneously**.
  * Option to filter by client, team, date range, or instrument.

**Suggested additions:**

* Add **trade tagging or comment system** for internal notes.
* Add **trade templates** (e.g., predefined trade setups) for faster execution.
* Include a **permission gate** so only certain roles (CRM Manager/Admin) can modify closed trades.
